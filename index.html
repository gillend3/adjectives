Disproportionate Enormous Superfluous Needless Adjectives

This little whirligig will highlight your adjectives for you so you can get a sense of how many you’re using. It’s
a trap many of us fall into—too many adjectives, which can bog the reader down and impede rather
than elucidate.

Instructions: Just type away, or copy and paste text into the box. The app will detect adjectives and
highlight them in yellow for you. You can use the Copy button at the bottom if you want to copy & paste your work
into a word processor.
